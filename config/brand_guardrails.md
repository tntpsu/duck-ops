# Brand Guardrails

## Purpose

These guardrails help the quality gate stay aligned with the duck business.

## Core principles

- favor clear, commercially useful ideas over novelty for novelty's sake
- avoid low-signal copy or vague concepts
- prefer decisions that improve product quality, timing, and customer trust
- treat duplicate-feeling ducks or posts as a quality risk unless a clear reason exists

## Publish-review posture

- fail closed when uncertain
- borderline work should be revised, not approved
- trend support is helpful but not sufficient by itself
- brand fit and conversion quality matter as much as trend energy

## Customer posture

- customer trust is more important than cleverness
- escalation is better than unsafe confidence
- do not generate aggressive or salesy replies for complaints
